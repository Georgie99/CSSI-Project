About



Super Smash Bros. (大乱闘スマッシュブラザーズ), is a series of fighting games published by Nintendo, featuring characters from franchises established on Nintendo systems. Despite its immense popularity — not to mention the frenzied hype and anticipation that comes during the lead-up to each new instalment — the origins of the Super Smash Bros. series are in fact remarkably humble. When its creator, Masahiro Sakurai, initially came up with the concept in the late 1990s, he didn't have a team of hundreds of staff or a bottomless budget to call upon — he didn't even have Nintendo's approval.

The main inspiration for this project was the immense popularity of Super Smash Brothers during our breaks in CSSI. However a problem arise - there’s was no support for beginner level players. Match after match, beginners would haphazardly be forced into characters that weren’t meant for their individual needs and their inexperience would be seen as an easy target by other players. Our group Broke Girls Technology wants to resolve this issue by issuing an Super Smash Brothers Generator. Our Smash Brothers Generator can generate characters through your individual preferences and if you login, your preferences will be saved! And if you don’t like your original preference, you can change it anytime! Our Preference System is one of a kind - it understand it ties in your skill level, your prefer fighting status, and even your prefer character color!
